About Me

Hi, I’m Samuel Babalola — a self-taught front-end developer and student of Industrial Technical Education (Electrical) at Yaba College of Technology, UNN affiliate. My journey into tech wasn’t handed to me — it’s something I’ve fought for. Where I live, electricity is often unreliable, so I code within the few hours of light I get each day. That challenge has only made me more determined. Debugging, problem-solving, and the satisfaction of finally making something work are things I’ve grown to love deeply. I started with simple HTML pages, and today I build functional web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with growing experience in Firebase. For me, coding isn’t just about writing code — it’s about finding solutions, learning resilience, and creating tools that can impact lives. Every project here is a reflection of my growth, curiosity, and drive to contribute meaningfully to the tech world.
